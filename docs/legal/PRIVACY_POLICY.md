## 8. Your Privacy Rights

### 8.1 GDPR Rights (EU Residents)
- **Right to Access:** Request copies of your personal data
- **Right to Rectification:** Correct inaccurate information
- **Right to Erasure:** Request deletion ("right to be forgotten")
- **Right to Restrict Processing:** Limit how we use your data
- **Right to Data Portability:** Receive your data in machine-readable format
- **Right to Object:** Oppose processing based on legitimate interests
- **Right to Withdraw Consent:** Revoke consent at any time

### 8.2 POPIA Rights (South African Residents)
- **Right to Access:** Access and correct your personal information
- **Right to Object:** Object to processing (with valid reasons)
- **Right to Deletion:** Request erasure of personal information
- **Right to Restrict:** Request restriction of processing
- **Right to Complain:** Lodge complaints with the Regulator (CIPC)

### 8.3 Exercising Your Rights
To exercise any of these rights:
1. Email: privacy@alliedimpact.co.za
2. Include: Your name, registered email, specific request
3. We will respond within **30 days** (GDPR) or **21 days** (POPIA)
